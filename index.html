Crop Collection

Crop Collection is a farming experience where your success depends on how well you manage your harvest and land. You will plant your fields, hire workers to manage your operations, and meticulously organize your inventory to maximize productivity.

Every crop you grow is a step toward completing your final collection, transforming a simple farm into a massive adventure. It's all about balancing inventory development with masterful use of the grid.
